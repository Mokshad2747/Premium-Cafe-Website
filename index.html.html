01 / The Philosophy

Rooted in Terroir, Refined by Hand.

We source single-origin estate beans from highly elevated tracts of the Western Ghats, roasting them micro-batch by micro-batch. Every cup served in our Colaba sanctuary is an active dialogue between India’s rich volcanic soil and avant-garde extraction standards.
